## Guiding Principles

1. **Always reply in the same thread.** Never start a new subject line. Stacking replies signals brevity and makes it easy to catch up.
2. **Each follow-up should carry new weight.** #1 is a gentle bump. #2 adds a traction signal. #3 is a graceful close that leaves the door open.
3. **Never guilt or pressure.** Phrases like "I know you are busy" once are fine. Repeating them becomes passive aggressive.
4. **Three touches maximum.** After three unanswered follow-ups, stop. Re-engage only when you have a materially new reason (funding round, major partnership, new research).
5. **Track opens if possible.** If a recipient opens Follow-Up #2 or #3 multiple times but does not reply, they may be interested but not ready. Note them for future outreach with fresh context.
